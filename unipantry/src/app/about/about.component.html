Who are we?

UniPantry is a startup founded by a group of
students who were frustrated with the traditional
experience of grocery shopping while attending
college. After hearing that many other students
also hated having to wait for buses, paying for
Ubers, and carrying around heavy bags in rain
and snow just to get healthy and affordable
groceries, UniPantry was born.

Made for Students by Students

We know that being a student is stressful, that's
why our goal at UniPantry is to give you the
most convenient shopping experience so you
can have more time focusing on what matters
most to you

Shopping Made Easy

Browse through the Unipantry website or, if
you are a member, use the app. Find anything
and everything you need at retail price or
lower. With Unipantry's personal shoppers, you
can order your apartment's groceries or your
secret stash of chocolate cake from the comfort
of your bed.

Order With Confidence

When you have everything you need, it's time
to head to checkout. With UniPantry, you can
get your groceries within two days. UniPantry
also makes it super easy to schedule repeat
deliveries for items you might need on a
regular basis. Have any special requests? You
can include shoppers notes with your checkout
so that your personal shopper knows exactly
what you want.

Fast Delivery You Can Trust

Go about your regular schedule and let
UniPantry handle all the time-consuming
shopping for you. Your groceries will be
delivered straight to your door within two days
(or whenever you've scheduled for them to
arrive). Grocery shopping has never been
this easy!

Backed by a Trusted Brand

All groceries are sourced from our partner, Hannaford Supermarkets so you know all
products you receive are of the highest quality for the lowest prices.
